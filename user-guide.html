🚀 Start Using Weekly Chronicles

🎯 What is Weekly Chronicles?

Weekly Chronicles is a social newspaper network where you and your friends share weekly stories in a beautiful flipbook format. Think of it as your personal newspaper where everyone contributes stories about their week!

🚀 Getting Started

Create Your Account

  1. Visit the Weekly Chronicles website
  2. Click "Don't have an account? Sign up"
  3. Enter your email, password, and full name
  4. Click "Sign up" - you'll be automatically logged in!
💡 Pro Tip: Use your real name and a valid email address - your friends will use your email to add you to their network!

📱 Main Features

📝 My Stories

Submit your weekly adventures with photos and choose whether it's a headline (major life event) or regular story.

Rules:
  • ⏰ One story per week
  • 📸 Up to 3 images per story
  • 🌟 Headlines appear first

👥 Friends

Invite friends by email to build your social newspaper network. They'll automatically become friends when they join!

How it works:
  • 📧 Existing users added immediately
  • 💌 New users get invitation
  • 👥 Up to 50 friends

📰 Weekly Edition

Read your personalized newspaper with stories from all your friends in a beautiful flipbook format.

Features:
  • 🌟 Headlines first
  • 📄 Page navigation
  • 📊 Story statistics

📚 Archive

Browse all your past weekly editions. Perfect for nostalgia and seeing how your friend network has grown!

Benefits:
  • 📅 Organized by week
  • 🔍 Easy browsing
  • 💾 All stories saved

📝 How to Submit a Great Story

Step-by-Step Story Submission

  1. Go to "My Stories" page
  2. Click "Write Story" button
  3. Fill out your story:
    • Title: Catchy headline for your story
    • Content: Write about your week (big or small!)
    • Headline: ✅ Check for major life events
    • Images: Upload up to 3 photos
  4. Click "Submit Story"

💡 Story Writing Tips

  • Be Personal: Share genuine moments from your week
  • Include Details: What, where, when, why?
  • Use Headlines Wisely: Save for truly special events
  • Add Photos: Pictures make stories come alive

🌟 Headlines vs Regular Stories

Headlines: Promotions, engagements, births, graduations, major travels

Regular: Weekly updates, small adventures, daily life moments

👥 Building Your Friend Network

Adding Friends

  1. Go to "Friends" page
  2. Click "Add Friend" button
  3. Enter their email address
  4. Click "Add Friend"
  5. They'll automatically be added to your network!

🎯 Network Building Tips

  • Start Small: Add 3-5 close friends first
  • Encourage Participation: Ask friends to submit stories
  • Grow Gradually: Add more friends as you get comfortable
  • Stay Engaged: Read and enjoy each other's stories

📅 Weekly Schedule

  • 📝 Stories Due: Monday night
  • 📰 New Edition: Tuesday morning
  • 📚 Archive: Available immediately

🏁 Quick Start Checklist

Your First Week

  • Create account and log in
  • Add 2-3 friends by email
  • Submit your first story (with photos!)
  • Check your Weekly Edition
  • Explore the Archive
  • Invite more friends to join

Weekly Routine

  • Check for new stories from friends
  • Submit your weekly story by Monday
  • Read the new edition on Tuesday
  • Add new friends occasionally
  • Share interesting moments with photos
